8+ Years Experience Procurement Tender Contract Vendor Management Dynamics 365 SCM Process Optimization

I'm Yeasin Siddik, working as Assistant Officer – Procurement at icddr,b, one of Bangladesh's leading public-health research institutions. My day-to-day covers IT goods and services procurement, full contract lifecycle management for a portfolio of roughly 150 active contracts, and compliance across major donor frameworks including USAID, GFATM, the Gates Foundation, GAC, UK FCDO, and Canada.

I also serve as the SPOC for the Microsoft Dynamics 365 SCM implementation at my organization — translating procurement workflows into digital, auditable processes. This work earned me an internal Procurement Officer of the Year recognition.

Alongside my career, I'm building LittleGrow Parenting, a Bengali-language brand for developmental toys and parenting books, and pursuing an MSc in Early Childhood Development — connecting my procurement and consumer-behavior background to how families actually choose products for their children.

Compliance & Contract Governance

Responding to Internal Oversight on a Non-Renewed IT Contract

Challenge

An internal oversight review raised questions about the non-renewal of a hardware supplier contract, requiring a fully documented justification.

Response

Drafted a formal, evidence-based response to the oversight team covering the procurement rationale, vendor performance history, and contract terms.

Outcome

Provided a clean audit trail that satisfied internal governance requirements without disrupting ongoing IT operations.

Vendor & Consulting Engagement

Structuring a D365 F&O Consulting Engagement

Challenge

The organization needed an external consulting partner for Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations, requiring clear scope and legal protection.

Response

Prepared a Professional Services Agreement and Notice of Intent defining deliverables, timelines, and obligations for the engagement.

Outcome

A well-scoped agreement that protected institutional interests while enabling the consulting partner to begin work without ambiguity.
