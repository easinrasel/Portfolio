ART. I
About
Two disciplines, one throughline
ES

I spend my working hours turning complicated procurement problems — donor compliance, vendor risk, contract terms — into clean, defensible decisions. It's a discipline built on evidence, process, and the quiet work of making sure things are done right the first time.

Outside that, I've become preoccupied with a different kind of system: how children grow, and how the products and habits around them shape that growth. That curiosity became LittleGrow Parenting, a brand for Bengali-speaking parents, and it's now pulling me toward formal study in early childhood development and psychology.

My longer aim is to bring the two together — using what I know about procurement, markets, and consumer behavior to study how families actually choose what they buy for their children, and eventually to teach and write about it.
